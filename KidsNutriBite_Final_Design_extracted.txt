KidsNutriBite – Final Data Collection & System Design (RAG + DB)
1. System Overview
We use a hybrid AI system:- Structured DB → for numerical data and filtering- RAG → for knowledge and reasoning- Planner → for calculations and diet plan generation- LLM → for final response generation

4. Team Data Collection Rules
- Extract numbers → DB- Extract explanations → RAG- One idea = one RAG chunk- Do not copy textbook directly- Maintain consistent food naming
5. Final Architecture
